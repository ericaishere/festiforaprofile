TOMORROW X TOGETHER is a bright and youthful boyband aiming to become the next global breakthrough artist. The band is composed of five members, SOOBIN, YEONJUN, BEOMGYU, TAEHYUN, HUENINGKAI, where each member is different from one another, shining in their own way to create synergy. They debuted on March 4th, 2019 with their first mini-album ‘The Dream Chapter: STAR. TXT Fandom Name is MOA (Moments of Alwaysness)
